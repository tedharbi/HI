WALA PANG TITLE

The sun shines bright whenever it's day,
But the day will be brighter if you choose me today.
The moon is beautiful and has a power to entice,
Yet not as much as powerful as those beautiful eyes.

The flowers in the field sway with the breeze,
They are given life by the butterflies and bees;
My body is made alive by your presence,
My sorrow has felt immediate evanescence.

Nature is truly such a wonderful thing--
But what I couldn't grasp is how I know such a wonderful being.
Thus have I decided to show my true feelings,
For what good would it do me, to bottle them up or let them flee.

I wonder if you notice what I do for you,
I wonder if you like the things I do,
But I'll do the best I can,
For such a perfect girl like you.
