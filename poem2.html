She's Art

No wonder I fell this hard,
Her beauty is comparable to none other,
As such I use a poem to describe her-
One that would last forever.

THer soul is pure and her spirit like glass,
Her body a testament to phenomenal class,
Her mind and strength are all admirable,
And her entirety is a blessing incomprehensible.

O what astounding light her beauty holds,
It shines through darkness,
And its power ever glows.

She must've been angel sent from above;
She has caught my gaze,
And now I can not stop.
What an amazing blessing it is,
To be able to witness a sight like this.

I wonder if we'll get to know each other,
A lot of boys and maybe I am just another-
But like a museum full of artistic pieces,
If I can not own the painting,
then I will just appreciate the art.
