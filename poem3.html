Harmony

As much as I love playing music,
I'd also love to play for you.
Every piece would become ever better,
To you, all of them I offer.

Of all sounds in this world,
I long to hear the sound of you.
Your voice would be music to my ears;
Your words would erase all my fears.

All the notes and melodies come together for you,
They tell us about the harmony we could make together.

Every instrument creates music,
And their unison creates fantasy,
My feelings for you are my way,
To create a musical piece called You and I.

There is an orchestra inside my heart,
And they all sing about you.
You are the most beautiful composition,
What honor would it be to become your accompaniment.
